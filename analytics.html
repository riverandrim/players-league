📖 Season Recaps

Four seasons. Four champions. Here's how it went down.

2022 🏆 Peter Lundquist — CHAMPION

Peter Lundquist's legendary 13-1 season ended exactly how it should have — with a championship. He posted a league-record 1,936.1 points for the year and rode a dominant two-week playoff run to the title. In a 4-team playoff, nobody could touch the inaugural champion.

2023 🏆 Kaleb Feahn — CHAMPION

Kaleb Feahn pulled off the ultimate Cinderella story — entering the playoffs as the 5 seed and winning three straight games to claim the title. Logan Rezac held the #1 seed all season but couldn't convert when it mattered. The first upset champion in league history.

2024 🏆 Noah Thesing — CHAMPION

Noah Thesing became the second consecutive upset champion, winning from the 7 seed with three dominant playoff performances including a 170.7 semifinal explosion. Logan held the #1 seed for the second straight year and lost in the first round — again. The #1 seed curse was officially born.

2025 🏆 Adam Stockwell — CHAMPION

Adam Stockwell broke the #1 seed curse, entering as the top seed and finishing the job with a dominant 150.3 championship performance over Mitchell Max. Meanwhile, Logan Rezac hit rock bottom at 4-10, earning the last-place punishment: wearing a dress to golf.
